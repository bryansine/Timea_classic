Timea-classic Returns & Refund Policy

At Timea-classic, we strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. While we primarily offer exchanges or replacements, we understand that certain situations may require a refund. Please read our policy carefully to understand your options.

Refund Details

General Refund Policy: We primarily offer replacements or exchanges. However, refunds may be considered under specific circumstances, such as:

  • If the product is defective or damaged upon arrival and a replacement is not available.
  • If we have shipped the wrong product.
  • If the product is unavailable after your order has been processed.

Refund Method: Refunds, when approved, will be processed using the original payment method. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

Refund Processing Time: Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.

Returns & Exchange Process

We do not offer cash refunds for change of mind. However, we are happy to consider a replacement or exchange as quickly as possible.

  • To initiate a return or exchange, you must notify us within 1 day of the delivery date.
  • The product to be exchanged or returned must be received by us within 2 working days of the delivery date to be eligible.
  • You are responsible for shipping/courier charges for a replaced/re-shipped product. In certain cases, at our discretion, we may offer free courier/shipping.

Conditions for Returns & Exchanges

Products cannot be returned or exchanged if:

  • They have been custom-made or cut to measure.
  • They have been damaged by lightning or other electrical surges.
  • They have been misused or intentionally damaged.
  • They have been used for a purpose other than their intended design.
  • They have not been used according to the user manual supplied with the product.
  • The original packaging, documentation, and contents are not intact or in new condition.
